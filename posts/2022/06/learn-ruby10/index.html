學習 Ruby 123 - 10 模組化

June 13, 2022

calc.rb

def bmi_calc(height, weight)
  weight / ( height * height)
end

p 'hello'

require#

main.rb

require './calc.rb'
require './calc.rb'
p bmi_calc 1.8, 60

會印出

"hello"
"hello"
18.51851851851852
18.51851851851852

load#

main.rb

load './calc.rb'
load './calc.rb'
p bmi_calc 1.8, 60

會印出

"hello"
18.51851851851852
18.51851851851852

小貼士#

  • require 不管載入幾次，第一次載入後，就重複使用了，所以只會載入一次
  • load 載入 n 次，真的會讀入 n 次
